Privacy Policy for Sneakers eBay App

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Sneakers ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our app to manage eBay inventory via OAuth integration. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and eBay's data protection requirements.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information you provide or authorize via eBay OAuth:

  • eBay user ID, username, and profile data (for authentication).
  • Inventory and order data from eBay (e.g., listings, sales, with your consent).
  • Device info (IP address, browser type) for security.

We do not collect sensitive information (e.g., race, health data).

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate and sync eBay data (via scopes like sell.inventory).
  • Provide app features (inventory management, alerts).
  • Improve services and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with laws (e.g., retain data for audits).

We process data only with your consent or legal basis (eBay OAuth grant).

3. Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell your data. We share only:

  • With eBay (as authorized via OAuth).
  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict contracts.
  • For legal reasons (e.g., subpoenas).

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for OAuth session. No third-party trackers. You can manage in browser settings.

5. International Transfers

Data is stored in US/EU servers (eBay compliant). We use SCCs for transfers.

6. Retention

We keep data as long as needed for services (e.g., until token revoke), then delete.

7. Minors

Services not for under 18. We do not collect from minors.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR/CCPA:

  • Access, correct, delete data (contact us).
  • Revoke OAuth consent in eBay Developer Portal.
  • Opt-out of sharing.

We update this policy; check date above. Major changes emailed.
